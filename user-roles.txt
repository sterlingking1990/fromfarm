fromfarm brings the freshness of farm produce closer to people who needs it at any time
eat fresh

UserRoles
1. enter farm
2. about fromfarm
3. gallery/news
4. buy farm produce (available on sign in)
5. sell farm produce (available on sign in)


4. Role
   1. choose to view more info about the produce to buy from a 3 column display of farm produce
   2. decide to buy 
   3. return to display of produce
   3. search farm by name of produce, category of produce, farm name, farmer

4.2 Role

   1. fill in a blank note of what you want
   2. send note to admin
   3. recieve notification with a pay button

4.2.3 Role
  
   1. pay and recieve delivery of farm produce

5. Role
   1. upload details about the produce you want to sell (farm name, produce type, category, note, image)
   2. send for approval by admin
